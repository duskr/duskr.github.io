Place your photos on the map

A marker is assigned to each photo, let's drag-and-drop!
If your image already contains GPS location, you can still tune it.
At the end, latitude and longitude will be saved in the dedicated EXIF metadata.

Synchronize with your tracks

You like to hike the mountains with a GPS in your backpack? With Locate, you can display your track and synchronise the position with your photos*.
Forgot to change the internal clock of your camera after landing in a diferent timezone? No worries, you can adjust a delay of ±12h with a 1sec precision and apply it easily to other pictures.
*Only with GPX and KML. GeoJSON is for display only.

Beautiful map layouts

Choose among five layouts and be sure to display the relevant information to place your photos:
Classic street style, hiking with topographic isolines, minimalist dark or light and satellite.
